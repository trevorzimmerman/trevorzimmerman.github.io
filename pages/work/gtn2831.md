# GTN2831 — Pick and Place System

**Role:** Lead Engineer  
**Project Type:** Automation / Control Systems  
**System:** Belt Loop Machine

I designed the pick and place subsystem for this belt loop manufacturing machine. I also developed the complete pneumatic system and the entire electrical system, including the control box, and all wiring.

---

Here is the full machine. My scope was focused on the subsystems in the lower third of the picture below.

## Pick and Place Subsystem

The system used:
- 3x Servo Motors
- Laser Sensor
- Proximity Sensors
- Photoelectric sensors
- Limit Switches

Here are design iterations of testing different fabric pickers:

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/zYPDavRviVY" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>

The most reliable and best economical choice was an off the shelf solution:

![Fabric Picker Operation (GIF)](/assets/projects/gtn2831/fabric-picker.gif)

The picker uses many needles to pick up the fabric. It can be adjusted for various types of fabric.

- Integrated sensors, servos, pneumatic solenoids
