# GTN2831 — Pick and Place System

**Role:** Lead Engineer  
**Project Type:** Automation / Control Systems  
**System:** Belt Loop Machine

Designed and developed the pick and place subsystem for an automated belt loop machine, including the mechanical mechanism, pneumatic system, and electrical control box.

The system used:
- 3x Servo Motors
- Laser Sensor
- Proximity Sensors
- Photoelectric sensors
- Limit Switches
