# GTN2831 — Pick and Place System

**Role:** Lead Engineer  
**Project Type:** Automation / Control Systems  
**System:** Belt Loop Machine

I designed the entire pick and place subsystem for this belt loop manufacturing machine. I also developed the complete pneumatic system and the entire electrical system, including the control box, and all wiring.

The system used:
- 3x Servo Motors
- Laser Sensor
- Proximity Sensors
- Photoelectric sensors
- Limit Switches
